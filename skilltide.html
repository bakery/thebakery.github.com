Get Help in Exchange for Travel & Hospitality

Every month, The Bakery will spend a few days helping out an entrepreneur or a startup, in exchange for travel and hospitality.

After picking a project, we'll setup a few skype calls to meet and greet, expose our skillset and suggest a rough scope of work. If things look good and the company/entreprenur can cover our travelling costs and arrange accomodation (a.k.a. startup camping), we'll setup a date and book the tickets.

The format will (usually) be of 3 days of work, followed by 2 days to explore the local whereabouts. During these 3 days, we'll usually start with a product audit and brainstorming session. This could include a UX review, design or technical suggestions, product strategy, growth & customer acquisition techniques, etc. The next two days consist of a hackathon to execute on the fruits of the first day's discussion. We might build a marketing widget, re-do your landing page, or create a rough prototype for your iOS app. But whatever happens, we'll ship something.

Send us a message. We'd love to talk.
