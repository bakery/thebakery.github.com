Current Project

We are currently busy trying to figure out how to build Airbnb for work. We want to enable all the creative professionals worldwide to be able to work in great companies in any part of the world for a few months at a time. Companies will get top international talent, while individuals will be able to have an amazing lifestyle without penalizing their professional growth.
