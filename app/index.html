Mission

At the Bakery, we believe there's a better way to work. We believe work, adventure and fun should not be mutually exclusive. We are constantly travelling the world, getting inspiration out of all new experiences and building things we are passionate about.

Wherever we go, we connect with developers, designers and entrepreneurs to feed our passion for learning and become better at what we do.

Current Project

We are currently building "Airbnb for work". We'd like to enable talented professionals worldwide to work for startups in other countries for a limited period of time in exchange for food, shelter and travel expenses.

Companies will get top international talent while individuals will develop their skills, networks and experience a new way of travelling and meeting people.
