What We Do

We are a team of passionate developers and designers dedicated to creating delightful online and mobile experiences.

We work closely with entrepreneurs and startups around the world to bring ideas to life and build engaging products and services. Our expertise spans from web & iOS development to product design, branding, growth-hacking and product strategy.

We are constantly travelling the world to work in-house with our clients, and get inspiration out of all new experiences. Wherever we go, we connect with developers, designers and entrepreneurs to feed our passion for learning and become better at what we do.

We have worked for startups in San Francisco, Paris, Hong-Kong, Croatia and Italy. Our clients include Y-Combinator and 500Startups alumni. Interested in working with us? Say hello.

Projects

  • CityBeat Berlin: The ultimate guide to Berlin's tech scene. We help tech professionals find places to work and people to work with in Berlin.
  • Openmic.io: "Give voice to images". Annotate pictures with voice. The easiest way for remote designers to get feedback.
  • Skilltide: "Airbnb for work". We'd like to enable talented professionals worldwide to share their skills while traveling in exchange for food, shelter or travel expenses.
